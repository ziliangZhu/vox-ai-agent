# VoxAgent

VoxAgent is a telephony runtime for AI agents. It helps AI builders connect existing text agents, workflows, and business systems to real phone scenarios.

[Official Website](https://vox-ai.teddymobile.cn/) · [Product Overview](https://vox-ai.teddymobile.cn/product) · [Request Trial](https://vox-ai.teddymobile.cn/trial/apply) · [Contact](./CONTACT.md)

## What Is VoxAgent?

VoxAgent provides the runtime layer between AI agents and real telephone networks. It is designed for teams that already have an AI agent, LLM workflow, RAG system, or business automation flow and want to make it work through phone calls instead of only web chat or browser voice demos.

VoxAgent focuses on phone entry, real-time voice interaction, runtime events, webhook integration, trial workflows, and production access preparation. Teams keep control of their own LLM, prompts, business logic, RAG, and tools while VoxAgent handles the telephony runtime layer.

## Who It Is For

- AI builders moving existing text agents into real phone calls.
- AI sales teams handling lead follow-up, qualification, appointment confirmation, and batch outbound calls.
- AI customer service teams building inbound support, callbacks, surveys, and multi-turn clarification flows.
- AI medical, education, legal, financial, and professional service teams that need long conversations and traceable processes.
- Product and engineering teams that do not want to build carrier access, number resources, SIP handling, voice runtime, and production access workflows from scratch.

## Core Capabilities

- **Phone number and call entry**: inbound calls, outbound calls, and batch outbound call scenarios.
- **Real-time voice runtime**: low-latency phone conversation support for long, multi-turn, high-interaction scenarios.
- **Runtime event model**: call lifecycle, ASR transcript, agent response, and call completion events.
- **Webhook integration**: connect phone events with existing AI agents, business systems, and automation services.
- **Developer portal workflow**: trial application, sandbox validation, production preparation, and access review.

## Common Scenarios

- **AI Sales**: lead follow-up, opportunity qualification, appointment confirmation, and CRM workflow integration.
- **AI Customer Service**: inbound support, callback handling, satisfaction surveys, and conversation summaries.
- **AI Medical Follow-Up**: medication reminders, recovery tracking, revisit reminders, and traceable follow-up records.
- **AI Education**: oral practice, trial lesson conversion, after-class follow-up, and continuous learning tracking.
- **Professional Services**: consultation triage, information collection, identity checks, and rule-constrained conversations.
